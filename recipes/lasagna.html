Lasagna

Description

Layer upon layer of rich, hearty flavor — that’s the magic of a good lasagna. Tender sheets of pasta cradle a savory meat sauce simmered to perfection, balanced with creamy béchamel and generous helpings of melted cheese. Every bite delivers the perfect marriage of textures: soft pasta, silky sauce, and golden, bubbling cheese with just the right hint of crisp on top. This is comfort food at its finest — the kind that fills the kitchen with an irresistible aroma and gathers everyone around the table. Whether served fresh from the oven or enjoyed as next-day leftovers (arguably even better), lasagna is a timeless dish that turns an ordinary meal into something worth savoring.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Ground Beef
  • 500g of Lasagna Sheets
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Sugar
  • 3 Large Tomatoes
  • 1 Large Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • 2 Large Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200 C and a grill to 200 C
  2. Cook the meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly.
  3. Add your Onion and continue cooking until the onions are translucent
  4. Add your tomatoes (cubes), garlic, parsley, basil, salt oregano and sugar
  5. Boil your lasagna sheets in lightly salted water until they are al dente.
  6. Mix your Cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, Eggs, Parsley, Salt and Pepper in a bowl.
  7. Layer the ingredients (sauce,meat,lasagna sheet) until your ingredients are up or your pan is full.
  8. Cover the baking pan with foil and cook for 30 minutes, uncover and cook for 15 more.
